## Contributor Guidelines

### First-Time Contributors

New to open source? We're happy to help! Look for issues tagged:
- `good first issue` - Great for beginners
- `help wanted` - We'd love your help
- `documentation` - Improve docs

### Regular Contributors

Frequent contributors may be invited to:
- Join the core team
- Gain commit access
- Help with releases
- Review pull requests

### Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are contributors who have:
- Made significant code contributions
- Demonstrated understanding of the codebase
- Shown commitment to the project
- Helped review pull requests
- Engaged positively with the community

**Interested?** Reach out to current maintainers!
